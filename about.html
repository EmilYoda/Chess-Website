About

This website was created as a university project and a tool for chess enthusiasts by 2 university students. Our goal was to create a decent chess website with functionality good enough for anyone to be able to use no matter their chess level. However it might still lack the necessary AI to give better responses and analysis for more experienced chess players, but the tools already implemented are more than enough for any beginner to learn on their own and understand the basics of chess and improve as a player. We hope to bring better features in the future and gorw our project.
